Kärt barn har många namn: AKA The Matthew Principle, and the 80/20 rule – 20% of the people will contribute 80% of the work. It holds for the number of stars in galaxies, for the sizes of trees in the rain forest and so on. This makes picking the winners extremely important. In a game of monopoly, it is life or death. Anytime you get discretization – a binary bottleneck, this is the case. It is a natural phenomenon, and free markets allow it. This also makes sense of ”striving for monopoly”. It shines a light on opportunity cost. In learning, it explains the ”Jack of all trades”.
